Mango Lassi

Description

Mango lassi is made with canned mango pulp blended with milk, yogurt, and ice for a smooth, creamy, refreshing Indian drink. Using mango pulp makes this recipe easy and avoids any chalky flavor you can sometimes get with fresh mangoes. Sprinkle ground cardamom on top if you like!

Ingredients

  • 2 (15.25 ounce) cans mango pulp, or mango slices with juice
  • ½ cup plain yogurt
  • ¼ cup milk
  • 2 cups ice cubes

Steps

  1. Blend mango pulp, yogurt, milk, and ice in a blender until smooth.
  2. Pour into tall glasses and serve.
